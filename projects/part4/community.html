Community & Events

Annual NVIDIA Pulse Hackathon, 2024

NVIDIA Pulse is a thriving global community of over 25,000 members passionate about real-time graphics, AI, and simulation. We host monthly meetups—both virtual and in-person—showcasing the latest breakthroughs in GPU acceleration and collaborative hackathons.

Join our Slack workspace to discuss optimization tricks, trade benchmarking results, and share your personal projects built with CUDA and Vulkan. Whether you’re a student crafting your first ray tracer or an industry veteran architecting multi‐GPU clusters, there’s a place for you here.

Upcoming events include the 2025 GPU Graphics Summit in San Francisco (June 15–17), AI & Ray Tracing Workshop at SIGGRAPH (July 20–22), and monthly “Code & Coffee” livestreams where we build demos from scratch.
