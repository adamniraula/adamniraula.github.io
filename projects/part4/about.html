ABOUT US

NVIDIA Pulse was founded in 2018 to bring together graphics engineers, game developers, and AI researchers under one roof. From day one, our ambition has been to showcase the latest in real-time ray tracing, deep learning AI, and professional rendering pipelines.

Over the years, we’ve grown into a thriving global community of over 25,000 members. Each month, our community publishes open-source tutorials, hosts live webinars with hardware experts, and shares best practices on forums covering everything from CUDA optimization to multi-GPU scaling.

Today, NVIDIA Pulse is not just a website—it’s an ecosystem. We partner with major tech conferences, run student hackathons for graphics hack-jams, and collaborate directly with engineering teams inside NVIDIA to preview next-gen GPUs. Our mission is to empower everyone—from first-time Linux users building a Vulkan renderer to seasoned engineers on the bleeding edge of AI-powered ray tracing.
