GeForce RTX 5060

The GeForce RTX 5060 is built on NVIDIA’s Ada Lovelace architecture, featuring 3072 CUDA cores, 12 TFLOPs of FP32 performance, 8 GB GDDR6 at 18 Gbps, and 24 RT cores for hardware‐accelerated ray tracing. Perfect for 1440p AAA gaming, creative workflows, and developing real‐time AI applications.

Key Specifications

  • CUDA Cores: 3072
  • Boost Clock: 2.30 GHz
  • Memory: 8 GB GDDR6 @ 18 Gbps

  • RT Cores: 24
  • Tensor Cores: 96 (4th Gen)
  • TDP: 150 W
  • Display Outputs: 3× DisplayPort 1.4a, 1× HDMI 2.1
